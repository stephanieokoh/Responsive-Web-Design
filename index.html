About

You Only Live Once.

Our Goal Is To

Live. Create. Expand

At our company, the goal is to become the largest multinational IT telecomunications company, a space where individuals can locate and acquire core networking skills and services they require. The achievement of our users and the celebration of your own personal progress drive your job as an employee.

I am in my final year of my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (Bsc.) at the University of Debrecen, Hungary. So far, my path has been nothing but a life changing journey having the opportunity to meet people from diverse backgrounds, experencing life and going through ups and downs with them. Looking back now, I don't think I would change anything. The entire process of my Computer Science degree was new to me, which made studying difficult at first, but I eventually got the feel of it.

As a result, I have always had the idea for this networking company due to my keen interest in computer networking. I want people who have the same drive and passion like I do find themselves on a new path and understand that telecommunication is also an interesting part of IT. Where people may learn and grow at their own pace while also excelling at the same time at the company.
